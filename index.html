Client Care & FAQ

Everything you need to know about your luxury order

How do I care for my Angel Jewellery?

To preserve the exquisite 24k gold plating and stone brilliance, avoid direct contact with perfumes, sanitizers, and water. Store each piece individually inside our provided anti-tarnish luxury box packaging.

What are your standard delivery timelines?

We process orders within 24–48 hours. Standard insured delivery takes 3 to 5 business days across India. Express courier options within Hyderabad can be coordinated directly over WhatsApp support chats.

What payment protocols do you accept?

Once your checkout list manifest is generated over WhatsApp, our executive concierge desk will verify real-time inventory slots and instantly share secure UPI handles, bank transfer paths, or custom QR links to settle your order booking safely.
